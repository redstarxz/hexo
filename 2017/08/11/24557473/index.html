Is there a difference between a few dollars and dozens of dollars of toothpaste?

August 10, 2017 本文总阅读量次

At present, toothpaste on the market can be divided into ordinary toothpaste and special effect toothpaste.


First of all, let’s first understand the ingredients of toothpaste

No matter what toothpaste, its main ingredients are friction agents, and other foaming agents, moisturizers, adhesives, aromatics, water and other additives, etc., to varying degrees of tooth massage, friction. Commonly used friction agents are calcium carbonate, calcium hydrogen phosphate and calcium pyrophosphate. Among them, calcium friction strong, more for the removal of stains and smoke stains.
How much is the difference between a few bucks and dozens of blocks of toothpaste?
Both pieces of toothpaste, or dozens of toothpaste, main ingredients are similar, are for the benefit of cleaning teeth, the effect of the difference is not particularly large. When buying toothpaste, we must not just look at the price, that the higher the price, and do not blindly believe that all types of advertising, because advertising is easy to exaggerate the unique ingredients.
Especially some drug toothpaste, in some oral diseases does play a role in mitigation, but is not a substitute for drugs, can not really put your treatment of oral diseases, so we should not be misled by these.
Toothpaste is mainly a supporting role, brushing method is the most important, followed by the toothbrush. Now many people turn this order around. Bleeding gums, periodontal disease is the swelling performance, to scaling treatment ah, with toothpaste is only temporary relief, cover condition, with oral disease, should be timely medical treatment.
Besides water, the main component of ordinary toothpaste is the following:
1, friction agent
2 surfactant (simple to understand is the cleaning agent or foaming agent)
3, fluoride
Other minor components include: pH regulator, stabilizer or adhesive, sweeteners, and some pigment. Efficacy type toothpaste, such as anti allergy, whitening, drug type, but also add the corresponding functional ingredients.


“
Puzzle one: toothpaste, the most important thing is to choose the friction agent?
Completely correct. The friction agent in toothpaste accounted for 30% - 55%, as a kind of insoluble particles by brushing the action, remove plaque and calculus from the teeth, to reduce dental caries and oral diseases.
Friction agent is calcium carbonate, calcium phosphate, aluminum hydroxide, and silicate hydrate (silica).
“
Puzzle two: the fewer bubbles, the better the toothpaste
Part right! Toothpaste has no problem with foaming agents. The problem is that it contains SLS foaming agents!
SLS is an even be added to the shampoo and Cleansing Cream will despise ingredients in toothpaste use is certainly not what good.
The use of SLS is supposed to make a protective layer of oral tissue becomes dry, and thus damage the oral tissue, increase the frequency of the occurrence of oral ulcers, can cause allergic cold teeth.
So well intentioned experts suggest better and less foaming toothpaste, refers not containing SLS or SLS content of toothpaste better. In fact, you don’t need to judge by blistering. You can see that the label on the toothpaste has “SLS free” or “SLS free”, and the logo is ok.


“
Puzzle three: fluoride in toothpaste is unsafe?
Incorrect! First, do not demonize fluoride, which is a naturally occurring mineral. Fluoride can strengthen tooth enamel and tooth mineral re.
ADA (American Dental Association was founded in 1859, professional dental organization, with China’s “national NCOH” is not the same ah) has been recognized by the effect of fluoride toothpaste, and all ADA certified toothpaste is fluoride toothpaste.
We only need to remember two points:
1, currently on the market most of the toothpaste will add a low dose of fluoride, recommend everyone with a fluoride toothpaste. Because low doses of fluoride strengthen the surface of the teeth, not only can they prevent cavities, but they also do little harm to the body.
2, fluorosis, dental fluorosis? Swallowing toothpaste and fluoride into the body can be harmful to health. Of course, I’ve heard of it, but it’s still the old saying that when you talk about the poison, you’re playing a hooligan. If you really want to count it, you’ll have to eat a whole tube of toothpaste to be harmful to your health. Therefore, even children should use fluoride toothpaste, as long as the control of the amount of toothpaste every time you go.
“
Puzzle four: About whitening toothpaste?
Whitening toothpaste mainly through these two ways to make teeth look white: one is through the removal of stains of tooth surface, as far as possible to restore the original luster; two is the whitening toothpaste paste is often blue, blue pigment can be temporarily attached on the surface of the teeth, make teeth to look more white, this is appearance oh. These whitening effect is only temporary, after stopping toothpaste, will restore the essence of the teeth.


“
Puzzle five: about gum bleeding, use xx toothpaste?
Bleeding gums, with XX toothpaste, advertising too much, think of bleeding gums subconscious: I need to change the hemostatic function of toothpaste. But, that’s no use at all! When brushing gums bleeding is warning: Master of dental calculus too much, need girls help me to a good.
Bleeding teeth this is the early symptoms of gingivitis or periodontitis, gingival shows in a very fragile state. If you use the hemostatic toothpaste, although the blood stopped, but your gums did not improve, dental calculus still exists. Thus, in the long-term stimulation of dental calculus, gingival recession, tooth root exposure, tooth loss, problems ensued. So, brush your teeth and bleed. Don’t worry about changing toothpaste. What you really need is love from the dentist.
“
Puzzle six: about toothpaste sensitive to teeth?
Anti sensitive toothpaste, there is a role for people with sensitive teeth. People with sensitive teeth have many small holes in their teeth leading to the pulp. And the pulp is the center of the tooth nerve, so these tubes can transmit cold, sweet and sour stimulation to the tooth nerve, causing tooth sensitivity and pain. These types of toothpaste form a protective layer on the surface of the teeth, sealing the tiny surface defects of the teeth, thereby relieving tooth sensitivity.


“
Puzzle seven: does toothpaste contain three chlorine? Is it safe?
Controversial, best avoided! Three chlorine (triclosan) is also a controversial ingredient in toothpaste. Three triclosan is an antibacterial agent that is commonly used in toothpaste, soap, hand sanitizer and medical disinfectant products.
On safety of three triclosan, the United States and Europe over the past 10 years has been a lot of controversy round, there is not a conclusion. However, say three chlorine will cause cancer, basically did not find any evidence, has been denied.
Why for three chlorine in toothpaste students without excessive worry?
Because the first, currently containing three triclosan toothpaste quantity is less than 10%, but from the amount, add the standard limit of domestic and American European consistent.
Second, from the manufacturer perspective, three triclosan is an expensive raw materials, there is no need to add too much.
But still suggest that, given the risk of three triclosan is relatively large, the effect is not irreplaceable, as the consumer must avoid containing three triclosan toothpaste!


Finally, not for a long time with a toothpaste toothpaste do not hold too long
Long-term use of a toothpaste can cause some harmful oral bacteria resistance and drug resistance, so that the toothpaste lost teeth sterilization effect, the best alternative use of toothpaste.
Toothpaste with longer exposure in the air more opportunities, and the contact frequency of toothbrush is higher, will increase the chance of exposure to bacteria.
The finally, toothpaste is mainly auxiliary function method and toothbrush is the main effect, only serious and correct brushing to keep the mouth clean is the hard truth!
