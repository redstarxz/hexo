We can see from the picture, Sun Yang and Ma child although looks more like (small probability events), but they are quite different in their genes (probability of events), after which led them after grow up, great differences in appearance: a handsome tall, is a sports genius, the other a plain, but also a full commercial genius. A rich variety of racial characteristics
Different genes regulate different characteristics.
For example, people’s dimples, dimples, people will be envious of many people, because they laugh will be very sweet. The dimples can also be inherited, but the genetic characteristics of dimple is very complex, sometimes they appear on our side of the cheek, may also be on both sides, it mainly depends on a gene, but it also will be affected by other genes. In addition, children with dimples when they grow up may disappear after they grow up.


On the whole, the inheritance of dimples is unpredictable, showing irregular inheritance.
Handedness is also determined by genes.
The researchers found that about 10% of people are left-handed, studies have shown that they are determined by about 1-2 gene, but another study found that the situation may be more complicated, there are about hundreds of genes to determine the traits of each gene to produce micro effect on this trait, the human brain development. Finally, the emergence of left handedness habits. Of course, cultural factors are also important in terms of handedness.


For example, the author’s sister was a left handedness when she was a child, but in the parents under the strict “tune”, she changed to do anything left-handed habits, slowly becoming right-handed handedness.

The study found that in the red hair crowd, this situation is more significant, different people face freckle distribution is different. This is because the genome carries a gene called MC1R, a dominant gene, so there is a further increase in the population.
In addition to these features, there are many other features of our facial features that make the world rich and diverse. In fact, the facial features and physical features are distinct in different races.
The difference in the looks of different races stems from their unique genes
In our minds, there is the impression that the Europeans’ noses are so high, why Africans’ hair curls, and why are Asian people relatively short?


In fact, such a mindset is not without any scientific basis. At present, the world has four major ethnic groups: Europa race, Mongolia race, Nigro race and Australian race. In the age of their life slash-and-burn cultivation, in different corners of the world, no genetic exchanges between each other, so each have very significant characteristics between different ethnic groups, which is adapted to the product of the environment. Such as the Oriental (Mongolia people) tend to have relatively flat face, thick lips, flat nose, body relatively little, while the Westerners (Europa) face recognition is very easy: high nose, deep eyes and thin lips.


Why are differences in facial features so striking?
This is because most human ancestors lived in Europe at that time, the winter is very cold, only those who have more high nose, nasal people can inhale cold air is heated sufficiently in the nasal cavity, not because of the cold and take more calories.


Thin lips, but also conducive to the preservation of Caucasian body temperature and heat of their own, instead of Oriental and African lips is relatively thick, in the hot environment, thick lips can help the body to the excessive heat loss. Asians maintain a smaller figure, but also for the same purpose.
Looks are genetically determined, and why are we not a copy of our parents?”
We can assume a situation, that is if we look exactly the same with our parents (50% of their genetic characteristics), then the world is monotonous and boring, there would be no people to pursue beauty. It is precisely because we inherited part of the facial features from our parents, but also have their own characteristics, so the world is rich and diverse.
Between yellow peas, green peas appear in the offspring, indicating genetic diversity, and offspring are rich in genetic traits. The pictures come from askabiologist.asu.edu
In fact, this involves a number of basic genetic laws. More than 150 years ago, a geneticist in Austria was trying to solve the problem. He is Gregory Mendel (Gregor Mendel), but he is not the object of study, but ordinary peas.


Mendel and his pea experiment, pictures from ck12.org
Mendel, a priest of an ordinary church, is unusual in that he is fascinated by the genetic characteristics of plants and hopes to find key factors behind genetic traits through breeding. So he opened a vegetable plot near the church for planting peas. After 8 years of arduous experiments, he found that the genetic characteristics of plants are heritable, different traits inherited, in a certain proportion, appear in future generations. This is the Mendel’s law of inheritance: the law of separation and the law of free combination.

For adults, too, in our body most complies with the rules of genetic gene Mendel, which is why we would like our parents, but they are not completely be “inherited” down, but was down part of the resulting offspring genetic, genetic feature rich and diverse, with our parents a distinction, rather than they completely pirated”.
