Why did Mao Zedong stop Lin Biao's Liberation of Hongkong in 1949?

August 10, 2017 本文总阅读量次

In 1949, Lin Biao field troops to Shenzhen, the 44 army in the Shenzhen Luohu bridge, in front of a river in Hongkong.
If this attack, less than three days, will be able to recover Hongkong. The British in Hongkong but ten thousand troops, and our army is qiuzhanyuwang heyday, fighting a strong country, as long as a charge, will be able to defeat the british.


The British are also very clear about this. The British governor Ge Lianghong that Hongkong would be liberated. At that time, the famous “Purple quartz” event happened again.
At the beginning of 49, our army ready to launch a campaign, say hello, foreign vessels from the Yangtze River, here to fight. As a result, the British frigate “Amethyst” did not mind, rushed into the blockade area. Admiral Liu Baicheng heard it, and sank it!
Our army shelled the unruly foreign warship and hit the “Purple quartz” on the ground. Britain sent three warships to rescue it and was also run.
Tell the British this thing, in foreign Chinese gunboat diplomacy ended. Chinese is for the invaders, people do not make me, I do not prisoners, if you make me, I say hello, if you make. Is that: hurried words without pre.
The British think this incident has made the British threat of force bankrupt. Britain naturally has no means of force intimidation on the Hongkong issue.
The British governor Ge Lianghong believes that the PLA troops do not even have, as long as the water with the broken food, finished in Hongkong for several weeks.
In fact, Mao Zedong not only did not enter, there is no water break food, Zhou Enlai even personally instructed to protect Hongkong’s drinking water supply.
So why isn’t Mao Zedong eager to liberate Hongkong?
Mao Zedong said, “Hongkong, he must be of some use.”.
Of course, after the event, Hongkong is of great use.
First, Mao Zedong used Hongkong to break the Anglo American alliance, breaking the blockade of the West against the new china. Britain announced its recognition of new China in 1950.
Second, not to recover Hongkong, but also make China no need to rely too much on the Soviet union.
Third, Hongkong has become a window for China’s external contacts, and China has imported a large amount of goods from Hongkong.
Fourth, to ensure the stability of Hongkong, and to promote the development of Hongkong since then.
Many young people in Hongkong now have a mistaken belief that the prosperity of Hongkong should be appreciated by the British, the colonial rule of Britain and the prosperity of Hongkong. This is really a misunderstanding. Hongkong’s prosperity is not supporting the British humanwell.
In 1949, Hongkong was only a medium-sized city, with a population of six hundred thousand.
At that time, Hongkong needed to look up to Guangzhou, not to mention Shanghai. It was precisely because of the liberation that Hongkong became the only window for foreign relations in the mainland. Of course, the people of Hongkong itself also efforts to seize this opportunity for a million, from a small fishing village into an international metropolis.
Hongkong can have today, the first to thank is Mao Zedong, as Shenzhen has today, Shenzhen people are most grateful to Deng Xiaoping.
And Mao Zedong to recover Hongkong, already had layout.
In 1974, Mao Zedong met with former British Prime Minister Heath, he did not go to Hongkong, such as Hongkong’s return, how to return, what happens if the problem occurs.
Mao Zedong didn’t care about it at all because he knew it wasn’t a problem. Hongkong is sure to come back, and I am the main, supplemented by the United kingdom.
Why?
In October 1971, China resumed its seat in the United nations. A year later, President Nixon visited china.
China has moved towards the world and no longer has Hongkong as an external window. Mao Zedong believed the British saw it too. At the time that Hongkong Sheehan can have a smooth return.
In the meeting, Mao Zedong calm and calm, said there are twenty-three years, this is not our thing, and then how to do, let them talk about it.


Mao Zedong points to Wang Hongwen and Deng Xiaoping.
It was Deng Xiaoping who succeeded in solving the Hongkong question, bringing Hongkong back to the motherland and ensuring prosperity and stability in Hongkong.
It can be said that the prosperity of Hongkong, before, thanks to Mao Zedong, and later, thanks to Deng Xiaoping.
