Although boxing gradually commercialized, boxing also slowly to the right track, but only 23 year old Shangguan Pengfei’s death, it is difficult for us to accept.


Shangguan Pengfei, is a Henan born Sanda athlete, born in 1988. Since childhood, like martial arts, in martial arts school acquired a skill, began the career road. He joined Henan Sanda Team, has won several champions, a war in 2009 is the most famous Chinese Martial Arts League 75 kg class competition, Shangguan Peng Fei beat the Guangdong team Dong Chuanchao won the championship, this let Peng Fei Shangguan fame.


On October 31, 2011, in the semifinals of the 80 kilogram class of Chinese Wushu Sanda Kungfu king, the opponent of Shangguan Peng Fei was Cui Fei of the armed police unit. The second round, Cui Fei hit the head side and then Shangguan Pengfei, Shangguan Cui Fei hold together, the referee had opened the opponent, Cui Fei has put boxing Shangguan hounaobiao, Shangguan Pengfei fell by our words, this is a black.


Shangguan Peng Fei immediately lost consciousness, fainted in the stadium, and then rushed to the hospital intensive care unit rescue, but because of head injuries, overweight, has been in a coma. In a coma for more than 20 days, the renal function gradually Shangguan Pengfei failure, the characteristics of life is more and more weak, after November 26th decreased respiration in December 12th, he stopped breathing, ended his 23 year life.


During this period of time Shangguan coma of his girlfriend, Pengfei, king of Shiyin he never abandon, always take care of him. When Shangguan Peng Fei died, the young girl suddenly burst into tears, crying almost fainted.” Touched countless net friend. Surprise, danger of such a big game, winning a prize is only 4000 yuan, and has won the national championship before the monthly salary of Shangguan Pengfei was less than five thousand yuan, an inventory of the people after the discovery of his heritage, the famous name left less than twenty thousand yuan deposit, the other is not what.


Because Shangguan’s family is not very good, in order to give him training and learning Sanda costs, father to go to the coal mine when coal workers to make money. However, after the death of Shangguan Peng Fei, the family still owed twenty thousand yuan of foreign debt. The death of Shangguan Pengfei caused a strong reaction from all walks of life. The official held that the incident was an accident and Cui Fei did not need to bear legal responsibility. Peng Fei led to the death of Shangguan Cui Fei, although not punished, but he can not stand the abuse and criticism of all, chose to retire.


In the high risk competition, the rules and the safeguard system of players are especially valuable. If the referee can stop Cui Fei’s behavior, if the treatment organizers can fully support the Shangguan Pengfei tragedy would not happen, maybe, maybe now only 29 years old, Shangguan is still active in the ring pengfei. We all want players to be healthy and safe while bringing us good games.
