On the same day, Jiuzhaigou scenic area received a total of 38799 tourists, of which 18158 teams, 20641 passengers alone. It’s not difficult to do that, because Jiuzhaigou is a scenic spot for fees and gates, and you can count the number of visitors per day depending on the number of tickets or the electronic gate data.
In contrast, the real difficulty, it is one that is free of charge area, especially without a barrier, how to get the number of tourists scenic spots.
According to the “tourism law”, the tourist attractions are required to publish the maximum carrying capacity of tourists. Passenger flow monitoring has always been one of the focal points of scenic spots. On the one hand, it is to avoid the potential safety problems caused by overloading passenger traffic, on the other hand, it is also the protection of tourism environment and resources.
West Lake scenic spots, for example, have been popular with tourists since ancient times. Zhang Daiceng of the Ming Dynasty complained, “West Lake, in the July half, there is nothing to see but to look at the people in the July.”. In 2002, West Lake became the first national 5A scenic spots free of charge. After the demolition of the wall hedgerow, West Lake administrative committee also regularly publish data of tourists.


West Lake tourists during the peak season
Estimation method does not rely on any equipment than the original is point measurement, generally at the entrance or area is an important tourist data acquisition point.
For example, in the National Day holiday travel peak, West Lake scenic area will send two security guards in the broken bridge on the number of heads. The thoughtfulness is that the staff will avoid the antipathy of tourists, only in mind. 5 minutes, roughly from the bridge to the end of the bridge, to collect the number of visitors on the bridge briefly. The 5 minute sample, multiplied by 12, is the total number of visitors in an hour. From 7 to 19, sampling 12 times a day adds up to the total number of visitors during the day. And night time from 19 to 7 when the amount of tourists, usually in the daytime 30%.
Card counting can only be compared to the scale, a rough estimate of the crowd, the infrared sensor is a more convenient and accurate method. This method is achieved by detecting the number of human bodies passing through the sensing area by infrared devices, and by measuring the resistance changes that block the infrared, or the specific infrared rays emitted by the human body, to determine the number of people. Two infrared receivers can be set up in the entrance and exit area to form a monitoring box, which can be judged to be “out” and “go” according to the order. The automatic change can be realized and the number of visitors can be counted in real time.
Infrared induction has the advantages of moderate cost and small equipment. However, this measurement method is sensitive to congestion, and the statistical accuracy is greatly affected by the location of infrared sensitive devices, and it can not distinguish whether or not to count repeatedly.
After the development of image recognition technology, video analysis has become the most mainstream method of passenger flow statistics. Video analysis not only can generate more accurate passenger flow data, but also can follow the moving track of the target, and form passenger flow.
Video analysis usually acquires images by vertical and high-precision cameras, and then judges population density based on image features. The population density is low, the image of man’s head and shoulders as feature points to determine the direct output of the recognition results; in the population density is high, can the image background area or to compute the trajectories of people. Video analysis method will be disturbed by the umbrella, shadows, dark floors and other factors, but the overall accuracy is very high, can reach more than 95%.


Can also be used to calculate the flow of shopping malls counting tools
In addition, in the current construction of scenic spots in the background, mobile phone signaling and Wi-Fi probe is also a common means of population estimation. Mobile phone signaling through operators to continuously track the user’s location and status; Wi-Fi probe is a mobile device identification open Wi-Fi function, can be detected without connecting equipment position. However, if a person carrying many intelligent devices, or is not open Wi-Fi function will cause the error.
Therefore, tourist attractions usually combine various means to estimate the amount of tourists and check and correct them.
However, the use of these statistical methods, in conjunction with the calculations for other large groups of people, is different from the standpoint of the statistical parties, and the results are also very different.

One of the reasons for the statistical discrepancy was that the organizers calculated the cumulative number, including those who left early before the end of the rally, while police posted instantaneous data for the night.
Among the number of people gathered, the most famous is the Jacobs Jacobs Method in the United States (Herbert). In 1960s, Professor Jacobs observed on campus in the office of anti war protests, the checkerboard cement thought of a simple method: calculate the number of samples in the grid, get the average number per cell, multiplied by the total number of squares, get the total number of approximate value.
So far, Jacobs’s method still occupies a guiding position in the number of meetings. Jacobs will “loose” crowd density defined in 10 square feet (0.93 square meters / person), “the crowd” at the density of 4.5 square feet (0.42 square meters / person), “the reception area extremely dense crowd” similar to a rock concert (mosh-pit) density is 2.5 square feet (0.23 / person square meters).
At present, the United States, South Korea, Singapore and Malaysia still use this method to calculate the number of meetings: the assembly site will be divided into grid, according to the degree of density, multiplied by the number of units per unit area. The accuracy of the method depends on the division result of gathering area and population density of judgment, however, with satellite images and Earth images with the help of Google, the statistical results accuracy has been steadily.
In addition, Korean scholars also calculate the number of candles according to the number of candlelight in the surveillance screen.
