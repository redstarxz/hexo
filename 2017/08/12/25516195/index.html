8 year old girl mistakenly swallowed No. 7 batteries, but parents gave her to eat steamed stuffed bun, drink honey?!

August 11, 2017 本文总阅读量次

August 7th,
Qingdao central hospital admissions a little sick
The 8 year old took a No. 7 battery swallowed them

That’s not the point,
To let the battery out as soon as possible,
Parents let children eat stuffed buns and honey…
Seeing this doesn’t work,
It took 4 hours to send the child to the hospital……
Once the battery falls into the intestines, it is troublesome
8 year old slightly (alias) is a special child, she is relatively slow intellectual development. In the morning of August 7th, grandma accompanied her to watch TV at home, and did not pay attention to the work, but slightly put the TV remote control battery out, playing in the mouth, accidentally swallowed.
Grandma found the urgency, and think about using folk prescription quickly let the battery out, first to the children ate two steamed stuffed bun, wait, and so did not respond, and from the Internet check for half a day, to the baby to drink honey, or not out.
Finally did not recruit, she gave a relative to work in the hospital to call, relatives heard a hurry, and rushed to send the child to the hospital. It was 3:15 in the afternoon, and it took 4 hours for the child to swallow the batteries.
Qingdao central hospital emergency center after receiving the patient, hastened to request gastroenterology consultation.


The battery position displayed on the gastroscope display screen.
“The child is not very good for her own special reasons, but obviously she is worried and nervous and has been walking up and down.”
The doctor let her down, because once the activity is large, the battery from the stomach into the intestines is in trouble, but the child refused to lay down the mood is not very stable, the mother and grandmother’s help, sit down is stabilized temporarily.
Food scraps keep out of sight
Abdominal film quickly confirmed that the battery is still in the stomach, which makes experts sigh, can not let the battery memory in the stomach for too long, otherwise it may happen unexpected accidents. Lou Yuangui, chief of the digestive department, immediately arranged emergency surgery and underwent endoscopic removal of the cells under general anesthesia.


At 5:30 p.m. that day, the operation began. When the gastroscope into the esophagus, experts shocked: “buns stuffed with depression, tomatoes, fruit residue.”
Under normal circumstances, the food enters the stomach, it will take 6 to 8 hours from the morning empty, the children eat the steamed stuffed bun has passed 4 hours, even without emptying, should also digest almost, but even the stuffing is very clear.
Lou Yuangui analysis: “May this child itself gastrointestinal peristalsis is relatively poor, she is 8 years old, weight already 90 jins, slightly fat, and in the case of mental stress, also can affect gastrointestinal peristalsis.”.”
There were too many scraps of food, and several solutions could not be put into practice. At first I wanted to use the gastroscope to suck out the mucus in the stomach cavity, but there were too many residues. The gastroscope would be blocked and inoperable. Then I would like to flush with water, but I worry about the food coming back.
In desperation, the doctor can only slowly find, 15 minutes later, finally in greasy food residue found below the hidden 7 alkaline batteries.
Fortunately, a surface of the battery is not broken
The battery is not easy to find, the doctor from the mirror to see the battery smooth no corrosion, battery smooth surface without the focal point, one end of Yuangui Lou gravity firmly fixed battery, a little bit to move, the child esophagus is relatively small, the lumen was narrow, in the process of move up in the battery slowly stand up, can be successfully removed.


Everyone held his breath and moved slowly up and down, lest the intensity should change slightly, causing the battery to slide. Ten minutes later, the swallowed battery was finally taken out.
