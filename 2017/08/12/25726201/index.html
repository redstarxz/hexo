What can I get for 100 yuan in India? Prices are low to tears!

August 12, 2017 本文总阅读量次


As one of the second largest population countries in the world, India has always been regarded as one of the most potential countries. Today I will show you the price of India and the standard of living of ordinary people.



1 yuan is about 10 yuan rupee, so the RMB is still very valuable in India.


One pound (500g) beef, as long as 9 yuan, beef liver, and even as long as 5 yuan.


Chicken wings, 1 jin, 6 yuan, much cheaper than domestic.


A packet of crisps costs as much as 2 yuan.


Both toothpaste and soap are one yuan each.


Potatoes and tomatoes are 3.5 yuan a pound, slightly more expensive than the domestic.


Seafood is very expensive, a pound of Pomfret 52 yuan, 25 yuan a pound of shrimp. However, compared to domestic, it is almost the same.


The service industry is very cheap. It is only RMB 3 yuan to cut hair at a street barber shop.


Interestingly, in India attaches great importance to female chastity, prostitution is cheaper to incredible proportions. Only 50 rupees each time, equivalent to about 5 yuan. 6 yuan spicy hot 13 times in India legend, perhaps really can achieve. As to why it might be so cheap, perhaps because of the demographic dividend, it’s sad to say.


To sum up, in India, 100 yuan =11 Jin beef, =50 packet potato chips = cut 33 times, head =20 times can not be described.


But the average wage of migrant workers in India is about 1900 yuan, and the monthly salary of low-income people is only 300 to 500 yuan. Moreover, there are still a large number of unemployed people in India. Therefore, in our view, very cheap prices, perhaps for the general public in India is not so cost-effective.
