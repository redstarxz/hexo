Many Liaoning fans only go to the scene to watch the ball once a year, the purpose is to scold Gao Lin

August 11, 2017 本文总阅读量次


In the just concluded in Liaoning Hongyun VS Guangzhou Hengda game, the final Guangzhou Hengda placid in the ratio of 3 to 0 win over the Liaoning hongyun. The game was not exciting, and the result was not unexpected, but there was an ugly phenomenon in the game that made us feel sick.



Every year, Guangzhou Hengda guest Liaoning Hongyun game will have a similar phenomenon, that is filled with the whole game of Guangzhou Hengda players Gao Lin condemning, this ugly phenomenon seems to have become a tradition, every year in the super stadium staged as usual.


The causes of things from the Gao Lin and Liaoning old grudges, and even can be traced back to the year 2005 Gao forest debut, but the real intensification of the 2011 Gao Lin in condemning the help of Guangzhou Hengda away goals more than 1 1 draw with Liaoning Hongyun, after scoring Gao Lin to the venue of the Liaoning fans who do. A sound and action, this action angered Liaoning fans who, since then when Gao Lin came to play in Liaoning, Liaoning will be “special attention” fans “”.


It further gaffe in 2015, when the Gao Lin team for the match against Liaoning Hongyun game after scoring again braved the rain, the sound can be heard without end to do a slapping action, which once again ignited the Liaoning fans who G-spot, Gao Lin condemning wavewave! And after the match, there was an ugly incident by Liaoning fans who abused Gao Lin’s wife in the channel.


There is no exception. Tonight, the Liaoning fans were shouting at Gao Lin all the time! But when Liaoning was 0 to 1 behind, the Liaoning fans are still called Gao Lin; when Liaoning was 0 to 2 behind, the Liaoning fans continue to scold Gao Lin; when Liaoning was 0 to 3 behind, the Liaoning fans who like to scold Gao Lin sound more tidy! Did these guys really come to watch the football match? I’m afraid not, but specifically for Gao Lin!


Curse it, as some netizens say, these people cherish the opportunity to curse Gao Lin, maybe next year no chance! By the way, even if Liaoning is downgraded next year and has no chance to play against Guangzhou Evergrande, these people can still talk about Gao Lin in front of the TV set! I’m too witty.
