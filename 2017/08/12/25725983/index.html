Wu Jing style "moral forcing""! Are "donors" all dark people?

August 12, 2017 本文总阅读量次

The expert: Yang Yuhui national two Counselors
At 21:19 on August 8th, 7 earthquake occurred in Sichuan County, Aba Prefecture, Jiuzhaigou. The stars of micro-blog to pray for the disaster, but due to the recent “wolf 2” netizens much attention Wu Jing also issued a document called: “pray for peace zone!”



Micro-blog, triggered a large number of users of the message, netizens to “wolf 2” at the box office on the grounds that “forced donations for the earthquake” Wu Jing.


Also a lot of friends just for Wu Jing, called the “forced donation” not in the moral kidnapping of Wu Jing.


In response to this, Wu Jing himself did not respond to these voices, in fact… Wu Jing has quietly donated one million!


So, why are there so many “forced donors”? What kind of psychology are they? Is it true that, as netizens say, is a moral kidnapping?
In the “forced donors” in the eyes of this matter, generally can be divided into three kinds of psychological:


1, your money is to earn everyone, so you donate money is tantamount to my contribution.
I have bought something on Taobao, so Ma money I have; I was in the cinema to see the wolf 2, so Wu Jingzuan’s money is my job.


So far, the logic is not a problem, but the robber logic: you earn my money, so I can be “God” as required to dispose of the money, so you money is my money. Letting others do what they are looking for and satisfying their emotions, which psychology calls “projection”, makes people feel at ease.


Altruistic Psychology: sociobiology believes that altruism is an instinct for animals to exchange their self sacrifice in exchange for the existence and continuation of species.” Instinct is what is in the genes that we depend on to survive. This later evolved into a theory of Psychology: when we see someone who needs help, we are willing to help, or psychology will cause anxiety.
2, if you have money, you must do good.
This is typical of the “absolute” cognition, there are many similar examples in real life: I must be admitted to a university, or my life will be over; I love you so you must be good to me, and I do not get married or not; I am your father, so you must listen to me…… Although it is a wrong cognition, it is stubborn and universal in the human brain.


We can only get rid of this erroneous cognitive control only by improving our self-cultivation, and constantly self-awareness, self reflection.


Value embodiment: psychologist Maslow put forward that the highest level of psychological needs of human beings is the realization of self-worth, which is higher than the physiological needs, higher social psychological needs. To realize self value, simply speaking, is to make contribution to society and obtain the affirmation of others. “Donation” is the realization of self-worth in a broader sense.
3, herd mentality, everyone is not strange a kind of psychology.
In the forced donation army, some people are wrong to recognize, they are “sincere” request star donations. At the same time, there is a considerable group, is “merry”: anyway, has been put forward, I followed shouted, if forced to donate is invalid, I do not damage what; but if you donate, it is under pressure or in the heart, I would think is forced anyway, so to experience pressure, “successful” feeling.
What? Morality? First I am not leading, second of our number, all you know? In the process of conformity, people’s moral sense will be reduced. This kind of “small investment, big profit” business is no wonder that attracts a lot of people to follow.


Herd mentality: social psychology holds that people tend to have a herd mentality, which refers to the tendency of people to adopt the behavior and opinions of other members of the group. Under the guidance of herd mentality, people tend to lower their ability to think independently.
How to treat Wu Jing’s “moral forced donation”?
First of all, recognize your heart. Happy in doing good Chinese is a traditional virtue, in the case of ability, to help more people, is a manifestation of inner cultivation, to the roses, hand a fragrance, not only benefit each other, they will also gain greater psychological satisfaction.


Second, recognize your responsibilities. Recognize their own social responsibility, “the greater the ability, the greater the responsibility”, this responsibility is willing to take. It is the burden, not the responsibility, that others impose.
Once again, recognize your role. Charity is their own business, since September 1, 2016 China’s implementation of the new “charity law”, the general called “charity event, refers to a natural person voluntarily carried out public welfare activities, from this point of view, no one can force you to do what.
There is no need to condemn a forced donor,
Because that’s their heart
There is no need to worry about being forced,
Just follow your heart
Everything comes from the heart
